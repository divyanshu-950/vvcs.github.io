OBJECTIVE : -

VOCS works on GVW ( Gross Vehicle Weight ) to Check whether the Vehicle is OverLoaded Or Not.

  • We are Helping Government in Reducing or Preventing the Incident Occur due to Vehicle Overloading.
  • We are Providing Vehicle Database which helps Drivers to know GVW of their vehicle
  • VOCS also Helps Drivers To Calculate that their Vehicle is Overloaded or not.

It also Provide a Feature, Where You Can Calculate the Maximum Amount of Load or Weight that Your Vehicle Can Carry and View Your Truck Maximum Load Capacity From the Database, You Can also Find GVM of Various Trucks Which Helps us to KnowMore About OurTrucks and Makes User Wary from The Risk of overloading Different Axle of their trucks.
